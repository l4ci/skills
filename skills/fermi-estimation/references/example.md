# Example: fermi-estimation

**You invoke it with:**
> "Give me a Fermi estimate of the annual UK market for electric cargo bikes used by last-mile delivery firms"

**It needs from you:**
- QUANTITY (required): annual revenue, in GBP, from new electric cargo bikes sold to UK last-mile delivery companies in 2026
- PRECISION NEEDED (optional): within 2-3x is fine; we just need to know if it's a £10M or a £100M opportunity
- KNOWN ANCHORS (optional): roughly 5,000 last-mile delivery firms operate in the UK, and a fleet-grade electric cargo bike runs about £6,000

**You get back:** a report leading with the estimate and its uncertainty band (e.g. "~£40M, plausibly £15-120M"), an auditable decomposition table, the two or three load-bearing factors, the dominant uncertainty, and an independent-second-path cross-check.

**See a full worked example:** [fermi-estimation-uk-cargo-bikes-2026-06-19.md](fermi-estimation-uk-cargo-bikes-2026-06-19.md), the report this invocation produces.
