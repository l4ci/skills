# Example: digital-maturity-assessment

**You invoke it with:**
> "Assess the digital maturity of our regional insurance business"

**It needs from you:**
- ORGANIZATION (required): Meridian Mutual, a 1,200-person regional property-and-casualty insurer with a 90-year-old core policy system and a two-year-old digital-claims team
- TARGET (optional but valuable): reach level 4 (Advanced) on customer experience and data within three years; the rest can sit at level 3
- EVIDENCE (optional): a mobile claims app live since last year, a data-warehouse project stalled at pilot, no product owners, and an executive team that still signs off every release

**You get back:** a saved markdown report (`digital-maturity-<organization-slug>-<date>.md`) with the seven-dimension maturity profile, per-dimension detail and evidence, the binding gaps and imbalances, and a sequenced roadmap of quick wins and longer plays toward the target.
