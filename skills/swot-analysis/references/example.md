# Example: swot-analysis

**You invoke it with:**
> "Run a SWOT on our meal-kit startup"

**It needs from you:**
- SUBJECT (required): "FreshFork, a regional meal-kit delivery startup serving the Pacific Northwest."
- OBJECTIVE (required): "Decide whether to expand into California within the next 12 months."
- CONTEXT (optional): "HelloFresh and a local grocery chain are the main competitors; tight margins; cold-chain logistics are our weak spot; a recent seed round gave us 18 months of runway."

**You get back:** a saved markdown report (`swot-analysis-freshfork-<date>.md`) with the SWOT grid, prioritized quadrant detail with magnitudes, the TOWS strategy matrix (SO/ST/WO/WT), ranked recommendations, and a verdict on the expansion decision.

**See a full worked example:** [swot-analysis-freshfork-2026-06-19.md](swot-analysis-freshfork-2026-06-19.md), the report this invocation produces.
