# Example: three-horizons

**You invoke it with:**
> "Map our product portfolio onto the three horizons"

**It needs from you:**
- ORGANIZATION (required): "Lumen Books, a mid-sized publisher whose revenue is mostly print trade books."
- INITIATIVES (optional but valuable): "Print trade list; audiobook imprint launched two years ago; a subscription e-reading app in beta; an early experiment with AI-narrated backlist titles."
- CONTEXT (optional): "Growth ambition is to double revenue in five years; the industry is shifting to digital faster each year; investment budget is tight outside the core."

**You get back:** a saved markdown report (`three-horizons-lumen-books-<date>.md`) with a horizon map placing each initiative by maturity, per-horizon detail and health, a balance-and-pipeline diagnosis, allocation and management guidance, and a verdict on whether the future is funded.
