# Example: analysis-of-competing-hypotheses

**You invoke it with:**
> "Run an ACH on what's really behind our Q2 enterprise churn spike"

**It needs from you:**
- QUESTION (required): what is the primary cause of the 40% jump in enterprise churn in Q2?
- EVIDENCE (optional): exit-survey responses, a competitor's price cut in April, a major outage on May 3, the support-ticket backlog, no change in NPS until June
- ROLE (optional): the VP of Customer Success, who must decide where to put retention budget this quarter

**You get back:** a saved markdown report leading with the ranked hypotheses and their relative likelihoods, the auditable scoring matrix, the linchpin evidence the verdict depends on, and indicators to watch that would change the answer.
