# Example: stakeholder-analysis

**You invoke it with:**
> "Do a stakeholder analysis for our move to a four-day work week"

**It needs from you:**
- INITIATIVE (required): "Piloting a four-day work week across the engineering and support teams for one quarter, with no pay cut."
- CONTEXT (optional): "We're in the proposal phase; the CFO is skeptical about coverage costs; the support team fears longer individual shifts; the CEO floated the idea publicly, so reputational stakes exist."

**You get back:** a saved markdown report (`stakeholder-analysis-four-day-week-<date>.md`) with the power-interest grid, per-stakeholder detail, a per-quadrant engagement plan, the coalition versus blockers, and the highest-leverage relationships to shift.
