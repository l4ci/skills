# Example: adkar

**You invoke it with:**
> "Run an ADKAR analysis on why field sales aren't adopting our new CRM"

**It needs from you:**
- CHANGE (required): moving the 120-person field sales team off spreadsheets onto Salesforce, six weeks after go-live
- GROUPS (optional): field sales reps, regional sales managers, the sales-ops support team
- CONTEXT (optional): a failed CRM rollout two years ago left reps cynical; managers are still paid on closed deals, not pipeline hygiene; exec sponsor went quiet after launch

**You get back:** a saved markdown report with a per-group scorecard (the five elements scored 1-5 with the barrier cell marked), the barrier point for each group, and a sequenced intervention plan with owners.

**See a full worked example:** [adkar-crm-field-sales-2026-06-19.md](adkar-crm-field-sales-2026-06-19.md), the report this invocation produces.
