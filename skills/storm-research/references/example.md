# Example: storm-research

**You invoke it with:**
> "Do a STORM-style deep research briefing on whether GLP-1 drugs reduce cardiovascular risk independent of weight loss"

**It needs from you:**
- TOPIC (required): "Do GLP-1 receptor agonists (like semaglutide) reduce cardiovascular events independently of the weight loss they cause, or is the benefit mostly mediated by weight?"
- ROLE (optional): "a primary-care clinician deciding when to prescribe"

**You get back:** a synthesis briefing (one-paragraph CEO summary, five ranked findings, a hidden connection, an actionable insight for the clinician, a frontier question) followed by a reconciled peer review with per-finding confidence scores and named gaps.
