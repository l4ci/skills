# Example: ocean-personality-test

**You invoke it with:**
> "Give me the Big Five personality test"

**It needs from you:**
- Nothing upfront — the skill administers the 50-item IPIP questionnaire conversationally (in batches of 10) and you answer on a 1-to-5 scale as you go.

**You get back:** a saved markdown report (`ocean-personality-test-<date>.md`) with a summary table of all five dimensions (total, per-item average, and band with a text bar), a per-dimension deep dive grounded in your actual answers, notable trait interactions, reflection prompts, and the non-clinical caveats.

**See a full worked example:** [ocean-personality-test-2026-06-19.md](ocean-personality-test-2026-06-19.md) — the report this invocation produces.
