# Example: six-sigma-dmaic

**You invoke it with:**
> "Run a DMAIC to cut the defect rate on our injection-molded phone-case line"

**It needs from you:**
- PROCESS (required): "The injection-molding line for phone cases. Roughly 8% of units come off with visible flash or short shots and get scrapped, up from about 3% a year ago."
- CONTEXT (optional): "We log scrap reasons per shift in the MES; the customer (our retail brand) requires zero visible cosmetic defects; scope is the molding cell only, not assembly; process owner is the second-shift lead."

**You get back:** a saved markdown project document (`six-sigma-dmaic-injection-molding-<date>.md`) covering all five phases (Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control) with proven root causes, a pilot result, a control plan, and a record that each phase gate was answered.

**See a full worked example:** [six-sigma-dmaic-injection-molding-2026-06-19.md](six-sigma-dmaic-injection-molding-2026-06-19.md) — the report this invocation produces.
